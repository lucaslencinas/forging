### Revenue Stream Details

#### Sponsorship & Advertising (~$1 Billion)
- Largest "core esports" revenue stream
- 60% of team/org revenue comes from sponsorships
- Growth slowing to 7% in 2025 (vs 18% in 2017-2018) - market maturing

**By Industry Sector:**
| Sector | Share | Examples |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Tech & Electronics | 28.7% | Intel, Samsung, Nvidia, Logitech |
| Food & Beverage | ~15% | Red Bull, Monster, Pepsi |
| Apparel | ~12% | Puma, Nike, Adidas |
| Automotive | ~10% | BMW, Mercedes, Honda |
| Financial Services | ~8% | Mastercard, Crypto.com |
| Telecom | ~8% | T-Mobile, Verizon |

**By Sponsorship Type:**
- Team Sponsorships: 35.4%
- Tournament/Event Sponsorships: ~30%
- League Sponsorships: ~20%
- Player/Influencer Deals: ~15%

#### Media Rights (~$370-600 Million)
- Streaming deals with Twitch, YouTube
- Exclusive broadcasting rights
- Growing at 22-25% annually

#### Esports Betting (~$2.8 Billion total market)
- Largest segment if included
- Only ~5-10% flows back to esports orgs
- Actual esports benefit: ~$150-300M

## Why Prize Pools Look Flat (But Industry is Growing)

### The Shift in Revenue Models

| Era | Model |
|-----|-------|
| 2015-2019 | Publishers pump money into prize pools as marketing |
| 2020-2024 | Money shifts to sustainable models |
| 2025+ | Esports operates more like traditional sports |

### Where Money Moved

| What's Growing | What's Flat/Declining |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Sponsorships (+20%/yr) | Prize pools |
| Media rights (+25%/yr) | Tournament count |
| Betting (+30%/yr) | Dota 2 TI pools |
| Player salaries | Individual game investment |
| Streaming revenue | - |

### Key Insight

> **"Prize pools are marketing spend, not industry health."**
>
> While prize money is $200-250M/year, the total esports economy is $3.2B -
> with sponsorships alone being 2-3x larger than prize pools.
>
> Teams and players make most of their money from:
> - Sponsorship deals
> - Salaries (funded by sponsors/orgs)
> - Content creation (Twitch/YouTube)
> - Merchandise

## Presentation Talking Points

### Slide 1: The Big Picture
> "The esports industry reached **$3.2 billion** in 2025, projected to grow to **$25.4 billion** by 2035 - an **8x increase** over 10 years."
